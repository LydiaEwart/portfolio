Personal Statement
I have a keen interest in music and art. I studied the piano at M.D. Rafferty Music School, where I am now a tutor. I have had the privilege of performing in a number of council events representing M.D. Rafferty Music School. My interest in art and technology lead me to complete a qualification in Creative Digital Media at Belfast Metropolitan College. Having an interest in cooking and baking I have developed my creative skills in cake and cupcake decoration for a number of different special occasions. I had a keen interest in sports while at school and am a past member of Tullyvallen junior Hockey Club.
